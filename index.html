Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

KeyWeave is a client-side web application. All typing drills are generated locally in your browser.

Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies used.

Local Storage: Your theme and preference settings are stored locally in your browser and never leave your device.

Third-Party Services: KeyWeave does not connect to any external servers or APIs.

Open Source: KeyWeave is fully open source. You can review the source code on GitHub.
